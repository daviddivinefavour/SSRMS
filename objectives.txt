The objective of the system are as follows:
Actors 
- Students 
     - Must be secured: should authenticate a student before allowing them into their student area.
     - See list of available courses for a semester.
     - Register Course: A student can register for courses in a semester. 
     - View Registered Courses: A student can view courses he has previously registered. 
     - View Results for selected semester after result must have been computed.
- Lecturers 
     - Secured Login
     - See course(s) allocated to him
     - See list of students registered for his course
     - upload students course results.
- Administrator 
     - Upload courses 
     - Allocate course 
     - Deprecate course 
     - View course list
